About us

Evitech, an ISO 9001 certified company aims to reduce carbon emissions across Australia by assisting residential and commercial customers through various methods such as upgrading inefficient lighting, installing solar PV and battery systems.

Greener, Cleaner, Better

Powering our lives shouldn’t impoverish our planet. This is the singular, unwavering belief that drives all our efforts here at Evitech. And that goal begins with reducing our collective carbon footprint.

We are leading the charge with a range of innovative, energy-efficient solutions that not only have the planet’s best interests at heart but also deliver significant energy savings.

Our residential and commercial solutions are rigorously designed and developed in Australia, are approved under the VEU and ESS scheme, and help create hundreds of local jobs.

Installation by A-grade installers

Evitech is proud to have a big network of local A-Grade installers. Our installers have flexible working hours which ensures that you face minimal disturbance during the installation process.
